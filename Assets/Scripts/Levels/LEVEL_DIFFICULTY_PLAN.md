# Level Difficulty Scaling Plan

## Difficulty Progression:
- **Level 1**: 20 blocks, 2 bolts - Tutorial (very easy)
- **Level 2**: 25 blocks, 2 bolts - Easy
- **Level 3**: 30 blocks, 3 bolts - Easy-Medium (slightly harder)
- **Level 4**: 30 blocks, 3 bolts - Easy-Medium (similar)
- **Level 5**: 35 blocks, 4 bolts - Medium (more platforms, more monsters)
- **Level 6**: 35 blocks, 4 bolts - Medium (similar)
- **Level 7**: 40 blocks, 5 bolts - Medium-Hard (longer, more obstacles)
- **Level 8**: 40 blocks, 5 bolts - Medium-Hard (similar)
- **Level 9**: 45 blocks, 6 bolts - Hard (more complex)
- **Level 10**: 45 blocks, 6 bolts - Hard (similar)
- **Level 11**: 50 blocks, 7 bolts - Very Hard (longer, more monsters)
- **Level 12**: 50 blocks, 7 bolts - Very Hard (similar)
- **Level 13**: 55 blocks, 8 bolts - Extreme (maximum complexity)
- **Level 14**: 55 blocks, 8 bolts - Extreme (similar)
- **Level 15**: 60 blocks, 9 bolts - Final Challenge (ultimate test)

## Platform Height Rules:
- All platforms: 1f height (cut by half from original 2f)
- Lightning bolts: 1f + 1.5f = 2.5f above ground
- Platforms spaced evenly based on bolt count

## Monster Placement:
- Level 1-2: 1 monster
- Level 3-4: 1-2 monsters
- Level 5-6: 2 monsters
- Level 7-8: 2-3 monsters
- Level 9-10: 3 monsters
- Level 11-12: 3-4 monsters
- Level 13-14: 4 monsters
- Level 15: 4-5 monsters
